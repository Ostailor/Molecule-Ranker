# Pilot Agreement Notes

These notes are inputs for counsel and pilot operations. They are not final legal
terms.

## Product Boundary

Molecule Ranker is for research use only. It is not medical advice, not clinical decision support, and not a regulated medical product.

The pilot must state that Molecule Ranker provides no patient treatment guidance,
no dosing, no lab protocols, and no synthesis instructions.

## Hypothesis Boundary

Generated molecules and antibodies are computational hypotheses. Molecule Ranker
makes no claims of cure, no claims of safety, no claims of efficacy, no claims of
activity, no claims of binding, no claims of manufacturability, and no claims of
developability.

Evidence and provenance should be independently reviewed by the pilot customer.

## Customer Responsibilities

The pilot customer is responsible for complying with laws, institutional
policies, and lab safety requirements.

## Commercial Terms To Define

- Pilot term length.
- Pilot fee and invoicing process.
- Authorized users.
- Usage limits and overage behavior.
- Support channel and response expectations.
- Data export and deletion on termination.
- Publication, publicity, and confidentiality.
- Security review and incident notification.
