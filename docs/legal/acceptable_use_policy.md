# Acceptable Use Policy

Molecule Ranker is for research use only. It is not medical advice, not clinical decision support, and not a regulated medical product.

Users must not use Molecule Ranker to:

- Provide patient treatment guidance.
- Provide dosing guidance.
- Generate or request lab protocols.
- Generate or request synthesis instructions.
- Claim cure, safety, efficacy, activity, binding, manufacturability, or
  developability.
- Present generated molecules or antibodies as validated actives or validated
  biologics.
- Treat generated molecules and antibodies as anything other than computational
  hypotheses.
- Bypass independent review of evidence and provenance.
- Violate laws, institutional policies, or lab safety requirements.
- Expose secrets, credentials, private integration settings, or another tenant's
  data.

Users are responsible for complying with laws, institutional policies, and lab
safety requirements.
