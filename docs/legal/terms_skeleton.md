# Terms Skeleton

This is a productization skeleton, not final legal terms.

## Research Use

Molecule Ranker is for research use only. It is not medical advice, not clinical decision support, and not a regulated medical product.

The service provides no patient treatment guidance, no dosing, no lab protocols,
and no synthesis instructions.

## Hypotheses and Evidence

Generated molecules and antibodies are computational hypotheses. Evidence and
provenance should be independently reviewed by qualified users.

Molecule Ranker makes no claims of cure, no claims of safety, no claims of
efficacy, no claims of activity, no claims of binding, no claims of
manufacturability, and no claims of developability.

## User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for complying with laws, institutional policies, and lab
safety requirements. Users must not use the service for clinical decision-making,
patient treatment guidance, dosing guidance, lab protocols, synthesis
instructions, or regulated medical use.
