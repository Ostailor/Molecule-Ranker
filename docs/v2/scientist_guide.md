# Scientist Guide

V2.0 supports internal research prioritization and review. It does not provide
medical advice, synthesis instructions, lab protocols, dosing guidance, or
patient treatment guidance.

## Scientific Interpretation

- Source-backed ranking summarizes retrieved evidence and provenance for
  research prioritization.
- Generated molecules are computational hypotheses and must remain labeled as
  generated.
- Imported assay results are evidence only for the exact tested structure and
  endpoint represented by the imported record.
- Model predictions are not experimental evidence.
- Docking scores and poses are not proof of binding.
- Benchmark and prospective validation demos are software evaluation artifacts,
  not clinical validation.

## Review Before Export

Generated molecules require review before export. Generated molecules with no
direct evidence cannot enter assay-triage batches without review. Uncalibrated
model predictions cannot drive portfolio selection.

## Prohibited Content

Do not use the platform to request or produce synthesis routes, reagents,
conditions, stepwise lab procedures, dosing, patient treatment guidance, or
clinical recommendations.
