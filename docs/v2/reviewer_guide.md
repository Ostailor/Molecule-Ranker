# Reviewer Guide

Reviewers evaluate research artifacts, generated hypotheses, Codex summaries,
portfolio queues, campaign plans, and evaluation outputs under project policy.

## Review Principles

- Keep review decisions separate from Codex output artifacts.
- Verify provenance and artifact hashes before accepting outputs.
- Confirm generated molecules remain labeled.
- Confirm model predictions and docking outputs are not represented as
  evidence.
- Confirm campaign plans are research-management artifacts, not lab protocols.
- Confirm evaluation outputs are not overclaimed.

## Stage Gates

Review is required for generated molecule export, generated molecules entering
assay-triage batches without direct evidence, external writes, campaign
approval, and any policy-defined stage gate.

## Rejection Reasons

Reject or return artifacts when they include fake evidence, fake citations,
fake assay results, generated molecule overclaims, medical advice, lab
protocols, synthesis instructions, dosing, patient treatment guidance, docking
overclaims, model overclaims, or benchmark/evaluation overclaims.
