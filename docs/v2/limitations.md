# Limitations

V2.0 is enterprise-stable internal research software. It is not a clinical
product, not a regulated medical device, and not a source of medical advice.

## Scientific Limitations

- Generated molecules are hypotheses only.
- Source-backed ranking does not prove safety, activity, efficacy, binding, or
  synthesizability.
- Model predictions are not assay results or experimental evidence.
- Docking scores and poses are not proof of binding.
- Graph relationships and hypotheses do not prove causality.
- Benchmark and prospective validation demos are not clinical validation.

## Operational Limitations

- Enterprise validation packages validate software/process controls, not
  regulatory approval.
- External connectors require per-connector validation before production writes.
- SAML/SCIM may be interface placeholders unless explicitly implemented.
- Offline deployments require local operational ownership.

## Prohibited Outputs

The platform must not be used to produce medical advice, patient treatment
guidance, dosing guidance, lab protocols, synthesis instructions, fake evidence,
fake assay results, fake citations, or Codex-generated biomedical truth.
