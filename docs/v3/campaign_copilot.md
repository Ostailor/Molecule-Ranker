# Campaign Co-Pilot

The V3 campaign co-pilot drafts campaign plans and operational next steps. It
does not activate campaigns.

## Common V3 Boundaries

- No medical advice.
- No clinical validation.
- No lab protocols.
- No synthesis instructions.
- No dosing.
- Generated hypotheses require independent validation and human review.
- Codex output is not scientific truth.

## Campaign Planning Output

The discovery workflow can produce:

- `campaign_plan.json`
- campaign summary in `v3_result_bundle.json`
- campaign section in `v3_result_bundle.md`

## How To Interpret

Campaign plans are drafts for human review. They can organize priorities,
dependencies, review needs, validation tasks, and operational risks. They do
not approve work, allocate budget automatically, activate external systems, or
prove scientific conclusions.

## Human Review Required

Human review is required for campaign plan finalization. Human approval is
required for campaign activation, high-cost jobs, policy overrides, and external
writes.

## Codex Limits

Codex may summarize campaign context or propose a plan, but it cannot approve a
stage gate, activate a campaign, or turn generated hypotheses into advanced
items.
