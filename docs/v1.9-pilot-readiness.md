# V1.9 Pilot Readiness

V1.9 prepares molecule-ranker for enterprise and internal pilot use. It is an
operational hardening release: usability polish, performance checks, reliability
controls, deployment diagnostics, monitoring, support workflows, support bundle
generation, pilot feedback capture, and pre-V2.0 readiness validation.

V1.9 does not add major science capabilities. It does not expand molecule
generation, docking, ADMET, external connectors, or predictive modeling except
for stability and usability work around existing surfaces.

## Boundaries

- Codex output is not evidence, assay results, molecules, scores, benchmark
  results, or decisions.
- Generated molecules remain computational hypotheses.
- Evaluation and benchmark outputs are evaluation artifacts, not biomedical
  evidence.
- molecule-ranker does not provide medical advice, lab protocols, synthesis
  instructions, dosing, or patient treatment guidance.
- molecule-ranker does not claim molecules are safe, active, effective,
  synthesizable, binding, or clinically useful.

## Validation

Pilot readiness is tracked by `molecule_ranker.platform.pilot_readiness` and
release checks. A pilot is ready only when all V1.9 operational areas have
evidence files and existing scientific guardrails remain intact.
